FRANKLIN COVEY

Description

At Franklin Covey, I had the opportunity to do UX/Graphic design work with the team in the creative lab as a contractor. Franklin Covey provides time management training and assessment services for educational facilities and/or individuals who are willing to learn. Some of Franklin Covey’s known works are the following textbooks that are currently being used in educational facilities today: “7 Habits of Highly Effective Principles”, “Find Your Voice”, “Inspire Others”, and “Take Charge.”

I worked on a project that involved taking the content of these textbooks and converting them to be an interactive course on an online platform. The courses were enhanced with interactive games, iconography, illustrations, and curated photos. Below I have provided some samples!

Working at Franklin Covey, I have gained experience on working with a team, being a self-starter on completing tasks, and working remote. I also have experience organizing group files, participating in meetings, and working effectively under pressure to accomplish meeting deadlines.
