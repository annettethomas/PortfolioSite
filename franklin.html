I worked with the Creative Lab of Franklin Covey as a UX/Graphic Design Contractor My role was a UX/Graphic Design contractor at Franklin Covey. I assisted by transitioning four published works onto a digital course platform. All images presented on this page is copyrighted and licensed by Franklin Covey. For more information on Franklin Covey, visit their main page.

Franklin Covey was developing an online course for high school students by converting their published textbooks into online digital courses. I assisted with the design by using their LMS (Learning Management System) with the following lanuages and software: HTML/CSS for content formating, Photoshop for imagery editing, Adobe Illustrator for illustrations, badge creation, icons, and H5P for interactive gaming exercises throughout the course.Below are samples of what I have created within the courses.
