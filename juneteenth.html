JUNETEENTH

Description

Betty Sawyer is a part of an organization that celebrates a national holiday every year called Juneteenth. She has a lot of information to share on her main website: projectsuccessinc.org, but wanted to make the topic of Juneteenth shine a bit more by dedicating a new website to it and needed some help with the design process.

Design

Betty wanted her website to be structured similar to the website: utahpride, but with theming that resembled African culture. Thus, the concept for the website was to have illustrations that expressed African heritage, and to use a moderate-toned color palette.
