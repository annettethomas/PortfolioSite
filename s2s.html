S2S

Description

I am currently working as a Web Designer at the Multicultural Center of Excellence department in the University that I graduated from; Weber State University. There, I design pages for a student-driven organization called Student2Student, an organization that informs and encourages high school students the importance of further pursuing their education and to help them transition to college if they are interested.

My job is to re-design the web-pages that are outdated and to ensure that students can easily navigate through the website and access the resources they need with ease.

Most of my work here is done using HTML, CSS, and Foundation.

Process

I first have a meeting with the department to determine what kind of content needs to be on the page, and the kind of style they are looking for when it comes to the design of the website. After the meeting I hand-sketch potential ways that the website could be designed, then will create a cleaner mock-up design through Figma. Once this is complete, the mock-up will be sent off to the intended department and we will go over any necessary adjustments if necessary.

Once we come to an agreement, I will start building the website using site manager, which is an official web content management system used create/edit websites and pages for Weber State.
